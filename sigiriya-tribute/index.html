Echoes of Sigiriya: A Tribute to Ancient Beauty

Legend has it that King Kashyapa built the fortress atop the towering rock to protect himself from his brother's wrath, and it was here that he housed his harem of beautiful women. The Sigiriya frescoes are a testament to the king's love for beauty and art, and their enduring legacy continues to captivate visitors from around the world.

Now, you too can own a piece of this ancient history with our exquisite homages of the Sigiriya frescoes. Our talented artists have recreated every detail with meticulous care, bringing to life the beauty and grace of these celestial women. Each piece is a true work of art, and a tribute to the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka.
